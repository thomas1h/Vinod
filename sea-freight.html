Sea Freight

Our sea freight department offer regular services for full containers loads (FCL), less than container loads (LCL) as well as break bulk and roro services to and from any major port in the world.

We are specializes in consol boxes to UK / USA / Mediterranean.

We offer an array of global ocean freight and transportation services. We deal in any size of shipment, from less-than-container loads to full container loads, special equipment and oversized cargo. With the use of advanced tracking facilities, we keep our clients informed about the status of the consignment during the transit.

Our Sea freight management services are known for their accurate and timely services between points of origin and destination. With reliability and price considerations, our sea freight services are cost effective with multiple weekly sailings. We offers shipments with fixed schedules under control at origin and destination. Our LCL shipments, FCL shipments and charter shipments are widely known for their time bound delivery.

Our international network allows us to handle shipments directly from terminal to terminal, or even from door to door.

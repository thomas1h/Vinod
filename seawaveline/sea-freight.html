Sea Freight

Sea Freight Services

Our Sea Freight Department offers regular services for Full Container Loads (FCL), Less than Container Loads (LCL), as well as Break Bulk and RoRo (Roll-on/Roll-off) services to and from any major port worldwide.

Comprehensive Ocean Freight Solutions

We provide a diverse range of global ocean freight and transportation services, accommodating shipments of any size, including:

  • Less-than-container loads (LCL)
  • Full container loads (FCL)
  • Special equipment cargo
  • Oversized cargo

Utilizing advanced tracking systems, we ensure our clients are informed about their consignment’s status throughout the entire transit process.

Reliable and Cost-Effective Sea Freight Management

Our Sea Freight Management Services are recognized for their accuracy and timely delivery from origin to destination. With cost-effective solutions and multiple weekly sailings, we guarantee:

  • Fixed schedules for shipments
  • Reliable service at both origin and destination
  • Time-bound delivery for LCL, FCL, and charter shipments

Global Shipping Network

Thanks to our extensive international network, we can handle shipments seamlessly, whether they require terminal-to-terminal transportation or complete door-to-door delivery.
